About Institute

Government ITI Karad provides technical training for students to build careers in industry.

Trades Available at ITI Karad

Electrician

This trade teaches installation, maintenance, and repair of electrical systems, wiring, motors, transformers, and industrial equipment. Students can work in industries, power plants, or start electrical work business.

Duration: 2 Years
Eligibility: 10th Pass

Fitter

Fitter trade focuses on machine assembly, fitting, drilling, and maintenance of mechanical equipment. Students learn workshop skills used in manufacturing industries, factories, and production plants.

Duration: 2 Years
Eligibility: 10th Pass

Welder

Welder trade teaches different welding techniques such as arc welding, gas welding, and fabrication work. Students can work in construction, shipbuilding, automobile, and fabrication industries.

Duration: 1 Year
Eligibility: 8th / 10th Pass

COPA (Computer Operator & Programming Assistant)

COPA trade provides computer knowledge including MS Office, internet, data entry, basic programming, and office automation. Students can work in offices, banks, companies, and IT sectors.

Duration: 1 Year
Eligibility: 10th Pass

Mechanic Diesel

Mechanic Diesel trade trains students to repair and maintain diesel engines, tractors, generators, and heavy vehicles. Students can work in transport, automobile workshops, and service centers.

Duration: 1 Year
Eligibility: 10th Pass
